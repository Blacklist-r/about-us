About

We document verified moderation incidents to help Discord communities identify and ban repeat offenders across servers. All reports include evidence, timelines, and recommended actions for cross-server safety.

Each report is compiled from direct evidence and witness testimony. We do not publish unverified claims. Our goal is to make Discord safer by sharing critical safety information between moderation teams.
